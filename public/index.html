Why Filipinos Love Mang Inasal

Mang Inasal has captured the hearts of Filipinos with its iconic **Unli-Rice** craze — a simple yet powerful gesture of generosity that resonates with every hungry stomach. At the core of every meal is the signature **Chicken Inasal** flavor: smoky, juicy, and unmistakably Filipino.

No visit is complete without a sweet ending, and Mang Inasal delivers with its beloved **Halo-Halo**, a Pinoy dessert favorite that cools and delights. Add to that the brand's **fast and friendly service**, and you've got a dining experience that feels like home.

With **affordable, value-packed meals** and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, Mang Inasal offers more than just food — it offers a taste of Filipino comfort, culture, and connection.

Our Story and Vision

Mang Inasal was born in **2003** in Iloilo City, founded by the visionary **Edgar "Injap" Sia II**. What began as a humble grilled chicken stall quickly grew into a nationwide favorite, now with over **500+ branches** across the Philippines.

From the very beginning, Mang Inasal has been committed to serving the **best grilled Pinoy food**, blending tradition with innovation to create meals that feel both familiar and exciting. The brand continues to redefine the **fast-casual dining experience**, offering flavorful dishes in a setting that's quick, comfortable, and proudly Filipino.

With a passion for progress, Mang Inasal is constantly innovating its menu and service — always listening, always evolving, and always serving with heart.
