Our Story

Mang Inasal began its flavorful journey in 2003 in Iloilo City, founded by the visionary Edgar "Injap" Sia II. What started as a humble grilled chicken stall quickly captured the hearts — and appetites — of Filipinos with its signature smoky flavor and unlimited rice offering. In just a few years, Mang Inasal grew from a single store into a nationwide sensation, opening over 500 branches across the Philippines.

In 2016, Mang Inasal joined the Jollibee Foods Corporation family, further strengthening its reach and impact. Today, it stands not just as a beloved dining destination, but as a symbol of Filipino entrepreneurial spirit. With every grilled chicken served, Mang Inasal continues to redefine local dining and inspire a new generation of homegrown success stories.
